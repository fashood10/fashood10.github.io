Winter kitty In the winter months, cats exhibit a fascinating
blend of resilience and comfort-seeking behavior. Their fur
thickens to provide insulation against the cold, giving them a
natural layer of protection. Observing cats in winter reveals
their love for warm and cozy spots, often curling up near heat
sources like radiators or basking in sunlit windows. Despite
their independent nature, many cats appreciate the extra warmth
offered by their human companions, seeking lap time or snuggling
under blankets. Winter also brings out their playful side as they
engage in brief bursts of activity to keep warm and entertained indoors. Click to read more
